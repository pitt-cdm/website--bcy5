Exploring Japan: One Bite at a Time

What is this?

This serves as a quick food tour guide to those who are either traveling abroad to Japan or plan to do so in the future.

Why is this guide special?

This guide organizes and categorizes each must-try eateries by its location. Each eatery is carefully chosen among the best of the best so that both quality and quanitity can be ensured at an affordable cost, so that YOU don't have to do the research.
